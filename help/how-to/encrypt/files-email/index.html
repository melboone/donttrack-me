Free, open-source alternatives to the proprietary services that are tracking you.

Opt out of global data surveillance programs.

There are many articles on what is and how to use PGP and GPG. This article will explain only the basics of both. Think of it as a... crash course.

PGP stands for Pretty Good Privacy https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pretty_Good_Privacy and GPG stands for GNU Privacy Guard https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNU_Privacy_Guard.

How does it work ?

Here is a very basic illustration http://www.pgpi.org/doc/pgpintro/ basics: http://www.pgpi.org/images/figures/fig1-1.gif si mai este una mai buna mai jos pe pagina. The public key is for everyone to see it and use it to encrypt documents for you, the private key is for you to decrypt de file.

The password ?

The password is your weakest point in encryption and as such the developers implemented passfraze. Here is an example of both of them.

Password: andrew123
Passfraze: Mama and me went to town to buy groceries yesterday.

How to use PGP/GPG.

Make your public key public. Publish your keys to the key servers so whoever wants to send you an encrypted file can find you with ease.
